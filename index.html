Our Story

True Worshippers Evangelical Ministries International was founded in 2005 by Pastor Michael and Sarah Johnson with a vision to raise up a generation of true worshippers who worship God in spirit and truth.

What began as a small home Bible study with 12 people has grown into an international ministry with congregations in three countries and outreach programs that touch thousands of lives each year.

Our Mission

To proclaim Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior, to make disciples of all nations, and to nurture true worshippers who worship God in spirit and truth.

Our Vision

To see transformed lives, thriving communities, and a world where every person has the opportunity to encounter Jesus Christ and become a true worshipper.

Our Core Values

Biblical Truth

We are committed to the inerrancy and authority of Scripture as the final authority for faith and practice.

Authentic Worship

We believe worship is a lifestyle, not just an event. We worship God in spirit and in truth.

Community

We are committed to building loving, supportive Christian fellowship where people can grow together.

Global Mission

We are called to reach every nation with the Gospel of Jesus Christ through missions and evangelism.

Compassion

We demonstrate God's love through acts of compassion, mercy, and justice in our community and world.

Discipleship

We are committed to making disciples who make disciples, nurturing spiritual growth at every level.

Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God and serve the world.

Sanctification

We believe in the ongoing work of the Holy Spirit to sanctify believers and empower them for ministry.

Christ's Return

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead.

Today's Devotional

Scripture Reading

1 Peter 2:9

"But you are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that you may proclaim the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvelous light."

Devotional Thought

As peculiar people chosen by God, we are called to live differently from the world. Our lives should reflect the character of Christ in every aspect. Today, remember that you are royal priesthood, set apart for God's divine purpose. Your peculiarity is not a weakness but a strength that showcases God's glory.

In a world that celebrates conformity, God calls us to stand out. Our values, priorities, and lifestyle should point people to Jesus. When we embrace our identity as God's peculiar people, we become living testimonies of His transforming power.

Prayer Focus

Heavenly Father, thank You for making me Your peculiar treasure. Help me to embrace my identity in Christ and live a life that honors You. Give me the courage to stand out for Your glory and the wisdom to represent Your kingdom well. May my life point others to Your marvelous light. In Jesus' name, Amen.
